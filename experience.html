MY EXPERIENCES

VISIT TO PARLIAMENT OF MALAYSIA

In October 2019, my friends and I participated in a visit to the Parliament of Malaysia. The visit was organized and managed by the Debate and Speech club from UiTM Machang. During this visit, we have gained experience of the culture of debate in the parliament hall. In addition, we have also learned a new discipline and rules that apply in the parliamentary constituency. For example, the strict ethics of wearing and entering and leaving the Parliament of Malaysia.

SUNRISE OF PULAU REDANG'S SEA FROM SEA VIEW POINT

This picture was taken by me in 2020 in the sea area of Pulau Redang, Terengganu. The experience of seeing the sunrise from the sea is very valuable as not everyone can go through this experience. The light of the rather cloudy sky and the newly rising sun from the east is an unforgettable sight when viewed from the sea. The cool air in the morning at sea gives a breath of freshness to those who go through this precious experience.
